Outreach & Education Specialist

Deliver community education, prevention messaging, and partner trainings focused on non-violence and equality.

Minimal Qualifications

Minimal: Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in social work, education, communications, public health, sociology, or related field (minimum)

Minimal: Certifications

  • DV/SA advocacy training (orientation + confidentiality)

Minimal: Experience

  • 1–3 years in outreach, training delivery, prevention, or community engagement

Minimal: Skills

  • Public speaking and facilitation
  • Curriculum / workshop development
  • Partnership building and community collaboration

Preferred Qualifications

Preferred: Education

  • Master’s degree in public health, social work, education, or related field

Preferred: Certifications

  • Train-the-trainer credential (if applicable)
  • Trauma-Informed Care Training

Preferred: Experience

  • 3–5 years leading trainings or prevention programs; grant/reporting experience a plus

Preferred: Skills

  • Experience working with schools, tribes, faith communities, or workplaces
  • Data tracking and evaluation (basic reporting)
