Executive Director

Lead mission delivery, partnerships, staff support, funding coordination, and survivor-centered program direction.

Minimal Qualifications

Minimal: Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in social work, public administration, nonprofit management, criminal justice, or related field (minimum)

Minimal: Certifications

  • DV/SA program leadership training (orientation + confidentiality)
  • Basic grant/compliance familiarity (training acceptable)

Minimal: Experience

  • 3–5 years leadership or supervisory experience in nonprofit/human services
  • Experience managing budgets, partners, and program operations

Minimal: Skills

  • Trauma-informed leadership and staff support
  • Strong communication and stakeholder management
  • Policy, confidentiality, and compliance mindset

Preferred Qualifications

Preferred: Education

  • Master’s degree in public administration, social work, nonprofit management, or related field

Preferred: Certifications

  • Grant management certification or training
  • Nonprofit leadership training
  • HR/management training (supervision, performance, policy)

Preferred: Experience

  • 5–8 years leadership experience; fundraising/strategic planning experience preferred

Preferred: Skills

  • Board relations and governance support
  • Program evaluation and reporting
