Mission Statement üíó

Our Mission

The Wholeness Center provides safety and services to victims and survivors who have experienced domestic violence, sexual assault, human trafficking, and stalking.

It is our goal to help these individuals make decisions about their lives in a supportive, non-judgmental, and empowering atmosphere.

We promote non-violence and equality as viable alternatives to aggression, domination, and control through education, advocacy, and social action.

Our History üåø

Founded in 1984

The Wholeness Center was founded in 1984 by a group of concerned citizens seeking to improve services and options for victims of domestic violence and families affected by critical issues.

Over the years, the agency has provided programs including a food pantry, safe homes, focused financial assistance, and access to community resources.
