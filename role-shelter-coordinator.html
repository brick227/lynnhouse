Shelter Coordinator

Coordinate shelter operations, daily logistics, and supportive services while protecting confidentiality and safety.

Minimal Qualifications

Minimal: Education

  • Associate’s degree in human services, social work, criminal justice, or related field (minimum)

Minimal: Certifications

  • DV/SA program training (orientation + confidentiality)
  • CPR/First Aid (or ability to obtain within 60–90 days)

Minimal: Experience

  • 1–3 years in shelter operations, human services, or related support role
  • Experience with documentation, scheduling, and crisis response protocols

Minimal: Skills

  • Strong organization, calm under pressure
  • Safety planning and conflict de-escalation
  • Team coordination and reliable communication

Preferred Qualifications

Preferred: Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in social work, sociology, psychology, or related field

Preferred: Certifications

  • Trauma-Informed Care Training
  • Food safety / basic facility safety training (if applicable)

Preferred: Experience

  • 3–5 years in shelter or residential services; supervisory experience preferred

Preferred: Skills

  • Experience coordinating volunteers and community partners
  • Knowledge of local housing/benefits/resources
