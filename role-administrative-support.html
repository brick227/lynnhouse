Administrative Support

Assist with scheduling, documentation, records support, and coordination to keep programs running smoothly.

Minimal Qualifications

Minimal: Education

  • High school diploma or GED (minimum)
  • Associate’s degree in business, administration, or related field (recommended)

Minimal: Certifications

  • Confidentiality & records handling training (required)

Minimal: Experience

  • 1–3 years in office administration, scheduling, or records support

Minimal: Skills

  • Strong attention to detail and professionalism
  • Comfort with basic office software (docs, email, calendars)
  • Discretion and confidential communication

Preferred Qualifications

Preferred: Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in business, public administration, or related field

Preferred: Certifications

  • Notary Public (optional, if needed)
  • Records management training

Preferred: Experience

  • 3–5 years administrative experience; nonprofit or human services experience preferred

Preferred: Skills

  • Basic bookkeeping or invoice tracking experience
  • Experience supporting HR/onboarding processes
